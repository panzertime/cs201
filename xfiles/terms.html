Terms of Endearment

A family seems to be under attack by the Devil. The police aren't buying it, and instead are blaming the father for the death of a newborn. However, it turns out that the father is himself a demon, and is trying to father a demon child. As the mystery unravels, it turns out that the wife is also a demon, and wants her own demon-baby, and has been framing the unknowing father all along.

I'm Only Happy When It Rains

When the demon parents are driving in their convertible, they play this song by Garbage. It's fitting because demons wouldn't be happy unless some misfortune was occurring.
