Ascension

In this episode, Mulder and Scully go to a tourist attraction atop a mountain because a large number of abductees congregate here regularly. It turns out that there's a plot to kill all the abductees (and Mulder and Scully), and the pair end up in great danger. The tourist trap, Skyland Mountain, is referred to multiple times throughout the series.

Red Right Hand

This song, by Nick Cave and the Bad Seeds, is about a murder, probably a killer for hire. Further in the episode, Mulder and Scully face off against a hired assassin.
