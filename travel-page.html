Travel to Samarqand!

Samarqand is an old Silk Road city located in remote Uzbekistan.

For centuries it was a central point in the East-West trade, and as such is a city with a vibrant culture and rich history.

Samarqand Facts

  • Inhabited for over 40000 years
  • Ruled by Alexander the Great and Genghis Khan
  • Timur's capital
  • Very hot
